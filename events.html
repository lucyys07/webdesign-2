🎭 William Shakespeare's Hamlet at the Alabama Shakespeare Festival

📍 Location: Alabama Shakespeare Festival | Festival Dr, Montgomery, AL 36117

🗓️ Date: January 24, 2025 - March 15, 2025

📝 Description: Dive deep into a timeless retelling of Hamlet, one of Shakespeare's most powerful tragedies. This spectacular production blends 21st-century innovation with traditional theater. Audiences will experience the iconic ghost, the gripping soliloquies, and the emotional intensity that make Hamlet a masterpiece.

💡 What to Know: Written between 1599 and 1601, Hamlet is considered the most famous play in the English language. Make sure to grab a program with behind-the-scenes insights.
